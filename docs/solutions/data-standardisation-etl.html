When you need this

Your Data Health Profiler score flagged high duplication rates or incomplete fields.

How it works

Entity Resolution

Deduplicates patient records across systems using probabilistic matching — so one patient is always one patient, regardless of how many systems they appear in.

Free-Text NLP Extraction

Extracts structured ICD-10, SNOMED, and LOINC codes from clinical notes, discharge summaries, and pathology reports. Unstructured data becomes queryable.

Completeness Scoring

Continuous field-level completeness metrics per data source. Flags which systems are producing incomplete records and quantifies the impact on AI model accuracy.

Schema Normalisation

Maps every incoming data format to a canonical FHIR R4 model. Drug names, units, timestamps, and identifiers are standardised before storage.

Duplication Removal

Automated detection and resolution of duplicate observations, orders, and records — including near-duplicate identification via semantic similarity.

Quality Gates

Configurable thresholds that block low-quality data from reaching AI models. Every record that enters the pipeline carries a quality score.
