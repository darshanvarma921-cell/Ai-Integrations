How it works

EHR Screen Embedding

AI recommendations surface as contextual panels inside the EHR screens clinicians already use. No new app to open. No context switch.

Audit-Ready Accept / Override

Every AI suggestion is logged with whether the clinician accepted, modified, or overrode it. Provides the evidence trail regulators and insurers require.

Role-Based Alert Routing

Patient flow predictions go to bed managers. Clinical decision support goes to the treating physician. Nursing alerts go to the assigned nurse. Signal reaches the right person.

Workflow Bottleneck Analytics

Continuous analysis of EHR audit logs surfaces where time is being lost — screen switches per session, handoff delays, documentation burden by department.

Low-Code Integration Builder

Configure where and how AI output appears without engineering involvement. Drag-and-drop trigger conditions, notification rules, and display templates.

Escalation & Fallback Logic

Defines what happens when AI confidence is low or the model is unavailable. Ensures clinical workflows are never dependent on AI uptime.
