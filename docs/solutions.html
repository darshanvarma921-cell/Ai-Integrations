A Deployment, End to End

Here’s what a real deployment looks like when every layer is in place — an ambient AI scribe, from connection to go-live.

01

Connect

Integration middleware identifies the patient and encounter, connects the scribe to the EHR, and opens a bidirectional data pipeline. No new vendor integration project — the connector layer already exists.

02

Prepare

Data standardisation ensures the patient’s history, medications, allergies, and prior notes are clean, correctly coded, and chronologically aligned before the AI touches them.

03

Embed

Workflow orchestration places the AI-generated draft note inside the clinician’s existing charting flow — right patient, right encounter, ready for review and sign-off. No new login. No separate screen.

04

Govern

The compliance engine logs which model version produced the note, what data it used, whether the clinician edited or accepted it, and maintains a full audit trail — reconstructable at any point.

05

Deliver

Managed deployment handled the readiness assessment, EHR integration, department pilot, staff training, success metrics, and optimisation before wider rollout. One accountable party. Not just software access — operational value.
