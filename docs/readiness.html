AI Readiness Assessment

A structured, evidence-based evaluation of your hospital’s readiness to adopt AI — across systems, data, governance, workflows, infrastructure, and people.

Six Domains of Readiness

Strategy & Use-Case Readiness

Does the hospital have clear priority problems worth solving with AI? We assess whether each potential use case has a clear owner, measurable outcomes, baseline KPIs, acceptable risk levels, and sufficient workflow volume.

Data Readiness

Usually the biggest blocker. We evaluate what systems exist, interoperability levels, data quality — completeness, duplicates, timestamp integrity — structure, representativeness, and access models.

Governance & Compliance

Consent and lawful basis for data use, privacy and auditability, model approval processes, clinical safety sign-off, human oversight, and monitoring for drift and bias.

Workflow & Operational

Where in the workflow AI output appears, who acts on it, staff trust, process standardisation, and fallback procedures.

Technical Infrastructure

Cloud policy, identity management, API capabilities, data lake maturity, model hosting, monitoring, and device integration.

People & Organisation

Executive sponsorship, IT buy-in, clinical champions, legal capacity, analytics maturity, AI literacy, and change management readiness.

What You Get

Overall Readiness Score— Single composite score across all six domains.
Domain-Level Scores— Strategy, data, governance, workflow, infrastructure, people.
Use-Case Readiness Scores— e.g. "AI discharge summaries: 82/100", "radiology triage: 54/100".
Gap Analysis— Specific deficiencies mapped to each domain.
Risk Heatmap— Visual representation of risk areas across the six domains.
Priority Roadmap— Sequenced plan for reaching AI-readiness, ordered by impact and effort.
Recommended First 3 Use Cases— Based on your specific system landscape and readiness profile.
Infrastructure Checklist— Technical requirements for deployment, specific to your environment.
Governance Checklist— Policy and compliance requirements before any AI goes live.
ROI Opportunity Map— Estimated value of closing each identified gap.

How It Works

01

Multi-Stakeholder Assessment

Role-specific questionnaires for CIO, CMIO, nursing leadership, legal, operations, and department heads. Evidence upload for policies, architecture docs, and vendor agreements.

02

Scoring & Analysis

Weighted maturity models across all six domains. Specialty-specific scoring templates. Automated extraction of readiness evidence from uploaded documents.

03

Report & Roadmap

Board-ready report with scores, gaps, risks, and a prioritised implementation sequence. Benchmark against peer hospitals. Recommended use cases matched to your readiness profile.

Most readiness assessments are self-reported questionnaires in a nice UI. Ours is evidence-backed, system-aware, and commercially useful. It maps your actual infrastructure, identifies your highest-value use cases, and creates the pipeline for integration — not just a score, but a blueprint.
